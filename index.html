Who We Are

Rooted in St. Pete.
Built for artists.

Lemonade Arts Foundation exists at the intersection of art, community, and civic life. We operate recurring public markets, gallery programming, immersive installation experiences, and artist development initiatives serving the full spectrum of creative practitioners.

Our long-term vision is a permanent cultural institution in downtown St. Petersburg — a museum and creative center that honors the city's artistic legacy and opens it to the world.

Our Vision

We're building the St. Petersburg we want to live in — a city where artists can afford to stay, where neighbors run into culture they didn't go looking for, and where the people who make this place interesting aren't doing it alone.
